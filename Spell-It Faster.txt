A word game where there are 3 diffictulties where words fall in a tetris shaped board and the goal is to type the word
before it hits the ground:
1. Easy
- 4 letter words

2. Medium
- 6 letter words

3. Hard
- 10 letter words

the game progressively speeds up.
the more words you get, the more points you get which goes on a leader board across other players too;
